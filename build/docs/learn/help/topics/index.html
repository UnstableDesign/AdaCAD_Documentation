Settings​

Set the Origin​

By default, AdaCAD assumes that all drafts begin in the top left corner (e.g. that the first pic and end of the weave are represented by the topmost and leftmost cell on the draft). You can change the origin of your drafts by clicking the the topbar.

Switching Drafting Modes​

AdaCAD supports two modes of drafting. In workspace mode, you generate drafts by creating dataflows. In the draft editor mode you can generate drafts following the traditional style of clicking cells on point paper. You can toggle between the two modes by selecting the corresponding mode name on the topbar of AdaCAD.

Additionally, you can move between the workspace and draft editor. You can edit any draft in a dataflow by double clicking the draft or clicking the and then selecting Open in Editor from the menu.
